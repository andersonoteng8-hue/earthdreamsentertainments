Our Story

Crafting Dreams into Reality

Earth Dreams Entertainment is a Ghana-based movie production company headquartered in Sunyani. The company specializes in producing high-quality, immersive film content designed to engage audiences while conveying meaningful social and human values. With a strong and growing presence across major digital platforms including TikTok, Facebook, YouTube, and Instagram, Earth Dreams Entertainment reaches a broad audience locally and internationally. The company is focused on talent development, film production, and the strategic use of visual media to educate, inspire, and influence society positively. Its notable productions include Kayayo, The Rich Poor Son, Tears by the Window, Day of Reckoning, and My Twin Husbands.
